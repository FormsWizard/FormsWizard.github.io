FORMS_wizzard

Forms Wizard provides you with secure data management and powerful analysis options - so you can focus your energy on collaboration and the future!

Flexibile Datamanagement

The Forms_Wizard aims to support crews in various use cases to collect and evaluate the data they need. This can happen self hosted or on our infrastructures.

Data evaluation made easy

A core part of the WizardPac is the evaluation tool - with the DataWizard you can visualize shift plans, show locations on a map, evaluate connections on network graphs & more.
Also we integrated an interdependence between dataset and visualization so that you can effictively make use of the different view ports.

Security by Design & Open Source Distribution

Security by Design is the leading concept in the WizardPac build. In combination with the Open Source distribution you dont need to trust your data into a black box.

Civic Engagement for Future

We believe in civic engagement - in times of increasing crises, not only in face of the climate catastrophe, we see the necessity of individuals organizing in communities. In that menner the whole WizardPac is transparently and collaboratively build as Open-Source.

In civic organizing contexts there are usually few resources and a lot of structure is needed to pool the collected energy - with Forms Wizard you wont need to worry about the digital security of your data anymore.
Learn more and follow the development process of this open source tool.
